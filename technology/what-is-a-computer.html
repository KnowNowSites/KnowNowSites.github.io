What Is a Computer?

A computer is a machine that can receive information, process it, and produce results based on instructions.

What does a computer do?

At a basic level, a computer takes input, works with that input, and then gives an output. The input can be text, numbers, images, or sounds.

Everyday examples

Phones, laptops, tablets, and desktop machines are all types of computers. They perform tasks like sending messages, browsing websites, or playing videos.

Does a computer think?

No. A computer follows instructions written by humans. It does not understand information the way people do.

Summary

A computer is a device that processes information by following programmed instructions to produce useful results.
